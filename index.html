Privacy Policy

Simple, transparent, privacy-first

Effective: June 1, 2025
Zero Data Collection

The Short Version: RailTracker doesn't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Everything happens on your device. That's it.

1.Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. RailTracker operates completely offline regarding your data.

  • No account registration required
  • No personal information collected (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad identifiers

2.Local Device Storage

RailTracker may save your app preferences (favorite lines, settings) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

3.Location Services

If you enable location features like "Nearby Stations," your location is processed entirely on your device. We never receive or store your location.

4.Public Transport Data

RailTracker displays information from official transport sources:

  • Transport for London (TfL)
  • Eurostar GTFS feeds (future support)
  • European transport agencies (future support)

We only fetch publicly available schedule data. No personal information is sent to these services.

5.Data Sharing

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to share with third parties.

6.Children's Privacy

RailTracker is safe for all ages. We don't collect information from anyone, including children under 13. COPPA compliant.

7.Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, you have rights regarding personal data. Since we don't collect any, these rights don't apply—which is the best protection.

8.App Store Compliance

This policy meets Apple App Store and Google Play Store requirements. RailTracker is designed to respect your privacy from the ground up. The app does not use any third-party SDKs that transmit data to external servers.

9.Crash Reporting

Currently disabled. If implemented in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect any changes.

10.Policy Updates

Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Check periodically for updates.

11.Permissions

RailTracker may request access to your location only to enable relevant features like “Nearby Stations.” This permission is optional and can be revoked at any time via your device settings.
